Guest Information

Accommodations

For the adventuresome guest, camping is allowed at the Rigmor House both Friday and Saturday nights! You are encouraged to bring your tent and sleeping bag and sleep out underneath the stars. Please let us know if you need camping supplies. There is a huge grassy field and a fire pit, so there will be plenty of space to accommodate everyone wishing to camp. Bathrooms and showers are available inside the Rigmor House, which will be open all night.

For those who would rather sleep in a bed (lame!), the official wedding hotel is the Hampton Inn and Suites:

Rental car information

Public transportation is limited in the Raleigh/Durham area. We will be providing a transportation option from the hotel to the wedding/reception site on Saturday night so you don't have to worry about celebrating and driving. Out of town guest will probably want a rental car to get around the remainder of the weekend. Car rental options at the RDU airport include: Advantage, Alamo, Avis, Budget, Dollar, Enterprise, Hertz, National, Payless, and Thrifty.

A search of rates (as of mid-January) showed the best rates for a two-day weekend rental (including taxes and fees) at:

  • Avis $125 Compact (use AWD K817165 for American Airlines or AWD K024700 for Southwest Airlines - you don't have to be flying either airline to use their discount)
  • Avis $139 Full-size (Use one of above AWD numbers and Coupon TUWA077 if you are going to rent for more than 3 days. The third weekend day is free with the paperless coupon).
  • Dollar $130 Compact (use code AAWT for American Airlines - you don't have to be flying American)
  • Dollar $128 Full-size (if you are a AAA member and use the appropriate code)
  • Thrifty $127 Full-size (if you are a AAA member and use the appropriate code)
  • Alamo $129 Full-size (using Contract ID 7016105 for Southwest Airlines and joining the free Alamo Insiders Club for an additional discount). The rate is $140 without the additional Alamo Insiders discount.

You can typically find additional discounts by pre-paying for your rental.
