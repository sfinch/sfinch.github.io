Local activities

The cites of Durham, Raleigh, and Chapel Hill (the Triangle) are all within driving distance of the Hotel and Rigmor House. Below is a short list of some of our recommended activities for those who are visiting the area. Most are located in downtown Durham, 15 minutes from the hotel and 30 minutes from the Rigmor House.
Restaurants
Here is a list of our favorite restaurants. North Carolina style BBQ will be served at the reception, so no BBQ restaurants are listed.
  • Cookout $ The ultimate NC fast food and late night dining experience awaits you. For under $5 you can get a cookout tray that comes with a burger, two sides, and a drink. A corn dog counts as a side. I repeat, A CORN DOG IS A SIDE. For 99 cents you can upgrade your drink to a milkshake, of which they have 38+ flavors. You can combine up to three flavors at no additional cost.
  • Dame's Chicken and Waffles $$ Southern comfort food. Waffles (classic, gingerbread, sweet potato, blueberry, or vegan) topped with crispy fried chicken and "shmears" (sweet crème butters).
  • La Vaquita $ Authentic tacos. No indoor seating.
  • Mateo $$ A great tapas restaurant in downtown Durham that also features a large Spanish wine list as well as one of the largest sherry offerings in the country.
  • Nana Taco $ Fancy tacos.
  • Shiki Sushi $ While this is only our second favorite sushi restaurant in the area, it's still pretty darn good and is conveniently located 5 minutes from the hotel.
  • Only Burger $ The only burger you'll ever want. Try the Fried Green Tomato Burger (Heather's favorite).
  • Pizzeria Toro $$ Wood fire pizza in downtown Durham. Although no reservations are taken and it's often crowded, this is easily the best pizza in the area.
  • Tyler's Taproom $ Get the Buffalo Chicken Sandwich and an order of the Carolina Nachos. If you don't, you ordered wrong. The Durham location is in the trendy American Tobacco district, next to the Durham Bulls athletic park. There are also locations in Chapel Hill and Raleigh. The Chapel Hill location is 15 minutes from the Rigmor House and 10 minutes from the Hotel.
Breweries
  • Big Boss Brewery One of the best Breweries in Raleigh, NC. They give tours on the second Saturday of each month at 2 PM, which includes April 11th! They also have a taproom for drinking.
  • Bull City Burger and Brewery Restaurant (serving...burgers) and a brewery. Located in downtown Durham.
  • Fullsteam Brewery A brewery located in a warehouse in downtown Durham. Great place for a group of people to meet and hangout. Food trucks are normally parked outside.
Other
  • Maple View Ice Cream Country Store Located 10 minutes from the Rigmor House, and open from 12-8 PM. Twelve permanent flavors are accompanied by seasonal specialties.
  • Sarah P. Duke Gardens and Duke University Duke Gardens are typically beautiful in the spring. There are five miles of walks and pathways throughout the Gardens. Duke Gardens also has a café, a gift shop, and a visitor center. The grounds are open from 8 AM to dusk daily, and admission is free. While you're at Duke, be sure to check out the rest of the campus.
  • Duke Lemur Center The Duke Lemur Center is the world's largest and most diverse collection of lemurs outside of Madagascar. A variety of tours are offered, with all tours by appointment only. To schedule a tour call 919-401-7240. Tours tend to book up 1-2 weeks in advance, longer for weekends during the spring and summer.
  • NC Museum of Natural Science General admission to this museum (located in Raleigh) is free, but there is a charge for special exhibits, such as the World's Largest Dinosaurs exhibit, which runs through April 12. There are three floors of exhibits, plus a Nature Research Center that opened in 2012.
  • Morehead Planetarium Check out the website for hours and prices of the various planetarium shows. Located in Chapel Hill on the UNC campus.
  • Shopping Mall at the Streets at South Point This isn't really a recommended activity, but if you need to do some shopping, this is the mall closest to the hotel/venue. It also happens to be the best one in triangle area.
