Accommodations

For the adventuresome guest, camping is allowed at the Rigmor House! You are encouraged to bring your tent and sleeping bag and sleep out underneath the stars. There is a huge grassy field and a fire pit, so there will be plenty of space to accommodate everyone wishing to camp. Bathrooms and showers are also available inside the Rigmor House.
